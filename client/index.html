Quotable - Source That Quote

"Use the Force, Harry" – Gandalf

That was the nonsense quote that motivated me to create Quotable. Probably you've seen similar stuff out there on the wild. I love them for what they are: jokes! But when I'm looking for a real quote, I want to be certain where does it come from. With Quotable you can find all kinds of quotes, even their source and context. And that's what makes it unique! Have you ever wondered where did a quote come from? Or why? It can be hard to grasp the whole message from some epic words. Context and source will help everything sink in. Join the movement and help us elimate all the annonymous quotes from the Internet. Let's source all quotes!
